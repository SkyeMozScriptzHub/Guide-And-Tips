How to Use Executors 🛠️:

  1. Choosing an Executor:
    • Executors are tools that allow you to run custom Roblox scripts safely.
    • Popular executors include Synapse X, Krnl, Script-Ware, and Fluxus.
    • Always download executors from official sources to avoid malware. ⚠️
    • Keep executors updated for Roblox compatibility.
  2. Installing Scripts:
    • Open your executor interface to paste or load scripts.
    • Copy trusted scripts (like from ZenoMoz Hub!). 📄
    • Paste and hit “Execute” or “Run.” ✅
  3. Safety Tips:
    • Never run scripts from untrusted sources.
    • Avoid scripts asking for personal info or account details. 🔒
    • Back up your account and important data.
    • Use separate Roblox accounts for testing scripts.
